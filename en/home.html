Online Classes Grouped by Age

0-2 Inspire Interest Children at 0-2 can gradually respond to simple commands, especially rhythmic songs, but they can only express limited words. It is also a period when a child’s gross motor skills develop quickly. So courses for children at this stage are mainly nursery rhymes added to interactive games which emphasize parent involvement.

2-4 English Rudiments Children develop more advanced language and communication skills during this period. Furthermore, children become more active and can participate in more physical activities. Thus, classes at this stage are mainly about nursery rhymes, picture books, and daily conversations with games. Parents should build a mimetic native language environment for children and create a condition of language output for children at this time.

4-6 Preschool Preparation English learning can also be a part of this stage of school readiness. Classes at this point mainly focus on daily conversations and phonics learning. Matching classic phonetic picture books to elaborate explanation, children are able to learn English easily from typical scenarios of everyday activities.
